Detection Factors

Urgency Indicators

Detects urgent language commonly used in fake posts

  • Keywords: "URGENT", "ACT NOW", "LIMITED TIME"
  • Patterns: Multiple exclamation marks
  • Behavior: Creating false urgency
Example: "URGENT! Click here to claim your $1000 prize!"

Financial Scams

Identifies posts promising money or prizes

  • Keywords: "FREE MONEY", "WINNER", "PRIZE"
  • Patterns: Dollar amounts, payment promises
  • Behavior: Unrealistic financial offers
Example: "Congratulations! You've won $500!"

Account Security

Analyzes account credibility and security threats

  • Keywords: "VERIFY", "SUSPENDED", "HACKED"
  • Patterns: Account verification requests
  • Behavior: Impersonation attempts
Example: "Your account will be suspended!"

Red Flags to Watch For

  • Posts asking for personal information
  • Urgent requests to click links
  • Promises of free money or prizes
  • Account verification threats
  • Suspicious URLs or domains

Signs of Genuine Posts

  • Natural, conversational language
  • No urgent or threatening language
  • Established account with history
  • No requests for personal information
  • Appropriate tone and context
