Detection Rules

These illustrative rules complement the ML model to enhance explainability.

Urgency Terms

Flags posts with words like "urgent", "now", "limited time" amplified by exclamation usage.

Prize & Giveaways

Detects common giveaway patterns and suspicious shortened links.

Credential Requests

Highlights prompts to "verify account" or request sensitive info via DMs.

Impersonation

Username patterns and avatars mimicking verified brands or officials.

Suspicious Links

New domains, misspellings, and non-HTTPS links are weighted higher.

Automation Signal

Repetitive posting cadence and templated content indicate bot-like behavior.
